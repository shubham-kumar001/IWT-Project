Dodo

Scientific Name: Raphus cucullatus

The dodo was a flightless bird endemic to Mauritius. It became extinct in the late 1600s, just a few decades after it was discovered by humans.

Quick Facts

Habitat: Island of Mauritius
Extinction: Late 1600s
Cause: Human hunting and invasive species
