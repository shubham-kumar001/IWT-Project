Saber Tooth Tiger

Scientific Name: Smilodon

The Saber Tooth Tiger, also known as Smilodon, was famous for its large, curved canine teeth. It went extinct around 10,000 years ago due to climate changes and the disappearance of large prey species.

Quick Facts

Habitat: North and South America
Extinction: Approx. 10,000 years ago
Cause: Climate change and prey extinction
Diet: Bison, camels, deer, tapirs
