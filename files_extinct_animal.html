Dodo

Scientific Name: Raphus cucullatus

The dodo was a flightless bird that lived on the island of Mauritius. It became extinct in the late 1600s due to human activity and introduced species. Food Consumed:Plant-based fiids,such as nuts, seeds, fruits, and roots

Woolly Mammoth

Scientific Name: Mammuthus primigenius

The woolly mammoth roamed the Earth during the Ice Age and became extinct around 4,000 years ago, likely due to climate change and overhunting by humans. Food Consumed: Plant based items like fruits, nuts, etc.

Saber Tooth Tiger

Scientific Name: Smilodon

The saber tooth tiger, also known as Smilodon, was known for its large, sharp canine teeth. It went extinct about 10,000 years ago. Food Consumed: Bison, Camels, as well as forest dwelling browsers such as deer and tapirs

Dinosaurs

Scientific Name: Clade Dinosauria

Dinosaurs dominated the Earth for millions of years before going extinct about 65 million years ago, likely due to a massive asteroid impact. Food Consumed: Some ate plants, Some ate meat, and Some ate both, but most were actually plant eaters

Titanoboa

Scientific Name: Titanoboa cerrejonensis

Titanoboa was the largest snake ever discovered, reaching lengths of over 40 feet. It lived around 60 million years ago in the tropical jungles of what is now Colombia. Food Consumed: Diet of titanoboa includes crocodiles, pythons, cobras, turtles, and large fishes.
